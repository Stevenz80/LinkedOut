Linkedout Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

Linkedout does not collect, sell, store, or share personal data.

What Linkedout does

Linkedout helps users summarize text they manually select on a webpage into a short, funny plain-English summary.

Selected text

Linkedout reads only the text that the user manually highlights after clicking the extension button. The extension does not automatically scan pages, read full browsing history, or rewrite webpages.

Local AI processing

Linkedout uses Chrome's built-in local AI features when available. The selected text is processed through Chrome's on-device AI system. Linkedout does not send selected text to Linkedout-owned servers.

Data collection

Linkedout does not collect analytics, tracking data, browsing history, account information, or personal information.

Third-party services

Linkedout does not use external AI APIs, advertising services, analytics tools, or third-party tracking services.

Affiliation

Linkedout is not affiliated with LinkedIn, Microsoft, Google, or Chrome.
